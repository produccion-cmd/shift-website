02 — Why We Start Here

Most projection installs go wrong before a single pixel is placed. Surfaces get measured wrong, throw distances get guessed, and the final look never matches what was imagined.

The result: the wrong projector, an image that doesn't cover the wall, or a quote that doubles once the real constraints surface. You end up spending more to fix it than it would have cost to design it right.

SHIFT uses Vectorworks Spotlight for precision venue modeling and Capture for real-time 3D previsualization. You approve the look in a virtual walkthrough before installation begins. The design phase fee is credited toward installation if you move forward within 30 days.
