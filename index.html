02 — How It Works
PRECISION
BEFORE
INSTALL.

Quoting blind means wrong equipment, surprise costs, and a result that misses the mark. We design the entire system before purchasing a single piece of gear.

01

Site Survey & Design

We measure the space, analyze surfaces, ambient light, signal paths, and mounting points — then design the complete system in Vectorworks. No guesswork.

02

Capture Previsual

The system runs inside Capture software — you see your actual space with real throw distances, real brightness, real feel — before anything is purchased.

03

Fixed-Price Proposal

One locked number. Not an estimate. Not a range. A precise installation quote that won't change because we've already designed everything it covers.

04

Install & Calibrate

Full installation, alignment, content load-in, staff training, and ongoing maintenance so the system performs every night — not just opening night.
